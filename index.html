What is cervical fluid?

Cervical fluid, often called cervical mucus, is a natural, healthy secretion produced by the cervix beginning at puberty. Regular cervical fluid self exams have some amazing health benefits—including preventative care, natural pregnancy prevention, and pregnancy assistance. But where does it come from?

Cervical Fluid & the Menstrual Cycle

The hormones estrogen and progesterone are responsible for noticeable changes in cervical fluid—including the look and feel—that occur during different phases of the menstrual cycle.
